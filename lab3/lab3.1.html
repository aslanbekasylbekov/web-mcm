The plot of the series "Money Heist"

"Money Heist", aka "La casa de papel" in Spanish, which means "Paper House" is a television series created by creator Alex Pina. It debuted on May 2, 2017 on Antenna 3. However, it became an Internet sensation only after being released on Netflix, which launched it internationally at the end of 2017.

Alex Pina is the creator of the show and also serves as an executive producer along with Sonia Martinez, Jesus Colmenar, Esther Martinez Lobato and Nacho Manubens.

The show revolves around a mysterious man who is known as a Professor and plans the biggest robbery in the history of crime. To carry out his plan, he recruits a team of eight people, each of whom is endowed with exceptional abilities, and they have nothing to lose. They intend to infiltrate the Royal Mint of Spain to print 2.4 billion euros. During this mission, they will have to spend 11 days in seclusion, dealing with sixty-five hostages and a highly effective police force.

The cast of the series "Paper House" is headed by Alvaro Morte, who plays Sergio Marcina or Professor. Other actors include Ursula Corbero in the role of Silenus Oliveira Tokyo, the narrator and the main character.

She is joined by Pedro Alonso as Andres de Fonoyos Berlin, deputy Professor, Paco Tus as Agustin Ramos "Moscow", Alba Flores as Agatha Jimenez Nairobi, Miguel Erran as Anibal Cortez "Rio" and Jaime Lorente as Daniel Ramos "Denver", Darko Peric in the role of Mirko Dragic Helsinki. They make up a team assembled by a professor, and each of them has a specific set of skills that they bring to the game. Also starring Itsiar Ituno as Inspector Raquel Murillo, Esther Acebo as hostage Monica Gastambide, Enrique Arce as director of the "Royal Mint of Spain" Arturo Roman and others.

After its release, the series gained such huge popularity that it became the most watched non-English-language series of 2018 on Netflix. He also won the 46th International Emmy Award for Best Drama Series in 2018.

On December 1, 2021, a spin-off of "Paper House" was announced, based on a character codenamed Berlin. The premiere of the spin-off "Berlin" is scheduled for December 2023.
