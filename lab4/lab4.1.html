5 Things To Do On Your Vacation In New York

New York - A city which reminds us of some friends laughing and spending time together in a café. A city where lovers meet at the Central Park to enjoy the sunset together. This city reminds us of dance, music, art and different delish cuisines. We may have never been to New York but we cannot deny from the fact that it is one of those cities where we all dream of cherishing at least some days of our lives.
If you ever imagine yourself wandering in the streets of New York and dreaming about the magic this city has for lovers, don’t stop yourself from this imagination. Close your eyes and take a long vacation to this magical city.
There is a lot to do over there. Let’s just start with the famous tourist attractions and an itinerary one can make during the stay in New York but even these won’t justify the beauty this city has to offer for the people and for the world.

1. AN EVENING AT TIMES SQUARE:

Times Square is one of the most recognizable places in the world. Compress New York in few blocks and you get the Times Square. An evening in Times Square is full of fun and lights. It is noisy and crowded with its own appealing beauty. Any night spent here is guaranteed to be one of the most memorable nights ever.

2. A VISIT TO THE STATUE OF LIBERTY:

The Statue of Liberty is undoubtedly one of the most iconic sights of New York. This beautiful and tall statue of about 93 meters high in the Liberty Island stands for its liberty and royalty and is visited by millions of tourists around the world.

3. A DAY AT BROADWAY IN THE THEATRE DISTRICT:

One of the interesting and top most activities to do in New York City is going to Broadway theatre in the Theatre District and watching amazing shows and attending startling theatres. The tickets have to bought in advance which depicts the worth and value of its shows.

4. A WALK IN THE CENTRAL PARK:

Lying in the heart of NYC, Central Park astonishes us with its magnificent beauty, crystal clear lake and lush green views. Go for a walk, breathe out all your worries, and lie down on the grass. It is no less than a visual feast for all of us.

5. A VISIT TO THE EMPIRE STATE BUILDING:

Visiting New York without seeing The Empire Building is like visiting Paris without going to Eiffel Tower. It is indeed one of the top tourist destinations of New York with the orange sky scaling the building and enhancing its tremendous beauty. Being at the top of the building not just let you enjoy the splendid views of the neighboring cities but also makes you feel like a part of you is there in those cities.
There are hundreds of other things to experience in this spectacular city. This list does not even do 1% justice to the panoramic experiences one can have on the vacation to New York so make up your mind, plan your vacation, fill up your heart with its beauty and go on a vacation that you will remember for a lifetime, I guarantee.
